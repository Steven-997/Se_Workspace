[ 1 ] Name 5 IT job roles and write one line about what each role does.
Here are 5 common IT job roles :

1. Software Developer – Designs, codes, and tests applications or systems to solve problems or meet user needs.

2. Network Engineer – Plans, builds, and maintains an organization's computer networks to ensure reliable connectivity.

3. Cybersecurity Analyst – Monitors systems for threats and vulnerabilities, protecting data and infrastructure from cyberattacks.

4. Cloud Architect – Designs and oversees cloud computing strategies, ensuring scalable and secure cloud infrastructure.

5. Data Scientist – Analyzes large datasets using statistics and machine learning to extract insights and support decision-making.








[ 2 ] List 3 product companies and 3 service companies with one example of work each does
Here are 3 product companies and 3 service companies:

--> Product Companies
(Companies that create and sell tangible or software products)

1. **Apple** – Designs and sells consumer electronics like the iPhone, MacBook, and iPad.
2. **Microsoft** – Develops and sells software products like Windows OS and the Microsoft 365 suite.
3. **Adobe** – Creates and sells creative software like Photoshop and Illustrator for designers and media professionals.


--> Service Companies
(Companies that provide expertise, skills, or labor rather than a physical product)

1. Accenture– Provides IT consulting and managed services to help businesses with digital transformation.
2. Infosys – Offers software development and outsourcing services to clients across industries globally.
3. IBM Global Services – Delivers IT infrastructure support and cloud management services to enterprises worldwide.
